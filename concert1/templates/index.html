Built by contractors for contractors...

If you hire or manage professional musicians, Concert Talent was built for you. Concert Talent isn't just a gig referral service, and not merely for self-promotion of bands and singer-songwriters. Concert Talent is a web-based and mobile app designed for the unique needs of industry professionals who assemble and manage musical ensembles of all types and sizes. Getting the job done fast, doing more with less, and operating globally is necessary for survival in today's rapidly changing music business. We're developing new features in response to input from major contractors all across the nation. This app assists you in leading your players while minimizing the administrative overhead that gets between you and the success of your next production. Sign up as a beta-tester, and help us build a custom-tailored product that makes finding and hiring musicians a joy.

What if I perform but don't contract?

Concert Talent is an app for professionals who hire musicians and/or who lead ensembles. If you do not hire other players, but are interested in getting on the roster of a particular contractor—or interested in connecting with a specific manager or presenter in a given city—feel free to sign up. If contractors using the app enable features allowing you to connect with them directly, you'll receive notice. In the meantime, we'll do our best to help you out and put you in touch with the right people. Whether you're a contractor or strictly a perfomer, we're here to help make life easier for all musicians!
